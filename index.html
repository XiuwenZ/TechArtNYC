our mission


We aim to promote the merging of technology and the performing arts. We hope to use the performing arts as a platform to expose and engage performers to visual arts technology. We want to break the mindset that the arts and computer technology are mutually exclusive.

Our Program

We use dance to empower, educate, and encourage girls as our next generation of engineers, scientists, and techies. Through our program young people of all ages will be able to build their confidence through dance and create challenging, technology-infused performances – ultimately building the skills needed for a future in STEM.
  • Our partners provide us with computers and ipads to help our students throughout the school year.
  • The talent program is our most popular program given to students at the top of their class. This is a very competitive program where only few people are admitted. Click below on “Learn More” for additional information.
